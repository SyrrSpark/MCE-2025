Massachusetts Cannabis Expo Job Fair

Secure Your Future in Cannabis — Build Generational Wealth by Getting in Early

FREE Entry Friday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Your Gateway to Cannabis Industry Careers

Many people think that breaking into the cannabis industry requires a business license or massive capital, but that's not the case. The beauty of this fast-growing industry is that you can start with a regular job and work your way up faster than in almost any other field.

Think about it: The tech boom, the oil rush, and the early days of banking all rewarded those who got in early—not just the investors, but the workers who gained experience before the industry matured.

The cannabis industry is still in its early stages, and those who position themselves now are the ones who will benefit the most in the long run. The MCE Job Fair is your chance to get ahead of the curve, establish yourself in a rapidly growing industry, and create long-term financial success that can be passed down for generations.

Why Getting in Early Matters

🚀 Ground-Floor Opportunities

The cannabis industry is projected to grow exponentially over the next decade. Getting in now means you'll gain experience, build connections, and grow alongside the industry.

💰 Build Wealth in a Billion-Dollar Industry

Early employees and entrepreneurs in emerging industries often see the greatest financial returns. Cannabis is no exception—this is your chance to secure a career that pays now and builds long-term wealth.

🏆 Be a Leader, Not a Follower

The industry is still forming, and the people who step up now will be tomorrow's executives, business owners, and decision-makers. Don't wait until it's too late—establish yourself today!

What to Expect at the MCE Job Fair

✅

Meet Cannabis Employers Ready to Hire

Connect with dispensaries, cultivators, processors, and ancillary businesses who are actively looking for talent. Whether you're seeking entry-level roles or management positions, this is your chance to get hired in real time.

✅

Responsible Vendor Training Providers (RVTPs)

Many cannabis jobs require training and certification. Get instant access to approved trainers who can help you meet industry standards and become job-ready faster.

✅

Expungement & Legal Assistance

Don't let past convictions hold you back. Experts will be on site to help you navigate the expungement process so you can reclaim your opportunities in the cannabis industry.

✅

Resume Writing & Career Coaching

A strong resume and interview skills can make all the difference. Career professionals will be available to help you craft a standout resume and prepare for cannabis job interviews.

Your Path to Generational Wealth Starts Here

🌱 Education, Experience, and Connections

Education, experience, and connections are the foundation of success. The cannabis industry is still young, and those who move now will benefit for decades to come.

💼 Ownership & Entrepreneurship

Many successful dispensary owners, cultivators, and executives started as budtenders, trimmers, or sales reps. Learn the business from the ground up, gain industry knowledge, and set yourself up for future ownership.

🏡 A Legacy for Your Family

Building wealth isn't just about the present—it's about creating opportunities for your children and future generations. Entering cannabis today means your family can benefit from your success tomorrow.
