Day 1 of the Western Massachusetts Cannabis Expo

FREE ENTRY TO ALL FRIDAY EVENTS
Friday Saturday Full Schedule

Friday Highlights

Friday is completely FREE to attend and features our Job Fair, Meet the Plug networking event, a financing panel, and the exciting Battle of the Budtender competition.

This is your opportunity to explore career opportunities, connect with cultivators, learn about cannabis financing, and witness exciting industry competitions.

Friday Timeline

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Speakers/Vendor/Sponsor/DJ Arrival

All vendors and sponsors can set up their booth, product activations, and brand displays.

Setup Period
10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Massachusetts Cannabis Expo Job Fair

The MCE Job Fair consists of employers, RVTP vendors, expungement services, and resume writing workshops. This is your chance to connect with potential employers and build your future in the cannabis industry.

Free Event Featured Event
Learn More
2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Meet the Plug

Meet the Plug is the only network event that allows the cannabis community a chance to meet the cannabis cultivators in western Massachusetts. Although there aren't any sales or consumption, the public can still build direct relationships with the people growing the cannabis they consume.

There will be a DJ, food is included in the vendor's fee, and access to water.

Free Event Featured Event
Learn More
4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

"Show Me the Money" Panel

Each speaker in this segment will have ten minutes to speak. At the end, the attendees will be allowed to ask questions for twenty minutes.

Panelists:

John Gilmore - SE Trust Fund Program Administrator

Kristen Glennon - BayCoast Bank Rep

Jill Grant - Sponsorship Collective

Free Event
6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

"Battle of the Budtender"

BOTB provides a platform for budtenders to showcase their knowledge and skills in a competitive setting. Players must answer 20 questions correctly. The first one to answer all correctly wins.

Prize: The grand prize winner will receive a BUD MASTER Champion belt and $2,500 cash.

Contestant Entry Fee: $50

Free to Watch Competition
