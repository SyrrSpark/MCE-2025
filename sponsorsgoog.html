Sponsorship Opportunities

Unlock Your Brand's Full Potential in Western Massachusetts' Thriving Cannabis Market

Why Sponsor the Western Mass Cannabis Expo?

The Western Massachusetts Cannabis Expo is your exclusive opportunity to showcase your brand, connect with industry leaders, and engage directly with consumers in a dynamic, immersive environment.

As a sponsor, you'll gain direct access to cannabis industry decision-makers, consumers, and community leaders in one of the fastest-growing markets in the Northeast. This is your chance to establish your brand as a leader in the Western Massachusetts cannabis landscape.

Our Audience

We target conscious cannabis consumers and industry professionals who are actively shaping the Western Massachusetts market.

Demographics

Target Age: 21-55 years old
58% Male, 42% Female

Millennials & Gen Z dominating purchases, with female consumers rising steadily year-over-year.

Consumer Motivations

Relaxation: 68%
Pain Relief: 54%
Creativity/Social: 47%
Sleep Aid: 39%

Product Preferences

Flower: 52%
Vape Pens: 24%
Edibles: 18%
Concentrates & Other: 6%

Purchase Habits

72% purchase at least once per month
40% purchase weekly

Key retail influence factors: Budtender recommendations, discounts, brand loyalty programs, and in-store promotions.

🎯 Custom Sponsorship Opportunities Include:

🔥 Event Segment Sponsorships - Own the Spotlight!

Make your brand synonymous with one of the expo's biggest attractions by sponsoring a key event segment:

  • 🎤 Battle of the Budtenders - Associate your brand with the most knowledgeable and competitive budtenders in the industry. 🏆 (Includes branding on championship belt & prize check)
  • 🚀 Cannabis Pitch Me - Connect with the next generation of cannabis entrepreneurs and position your company as a champion of innovation.
  • 💰 Show Me the Money - Sponsor the financial and investment panel where entrepreneurs learn how to fund their businesses.

Benefits include:

  • Sponsor Name Recognition in all marketing materials
  • Branded signage & banners at the event segment
  • Live mentions & speaking opportunities during the segment

🛋 Branded Lounges & Relaxation Areas - Where Attendees Connect & Recharge!

Create a high-touch brand experience with a customized lounge that offers attendees a space to network, relax, and engage with your brand.

  • 🏕 VIP Networking Lounge - Sponsor an exclusive networking space for industry leaders, investors, and speakers.
  • 💨 Consumption-Free Chill Lounge - A stylish lounge where attendees can enjoy the atmosphere in a relaxed setting.
  • 🎧 Podcast & Media Lounge - Be the presenting sponsor of a fully branded lounge where live interviews, media coverage, and content creation take place.

Benefits include:

  • Brand logo prominently displayed on lounge furniture, signage & digital materials
  • Branded giveaways (cushions, water bottles, lanyards, etc.)
  • Opportunity to distribute marketing materials or product samples

📲 Technology Sponsorships - Be the Power Behind the Event!

  • 🔌 Mobile Charging Stations - Attendees need to stay powered up! Have your brand logo displayed at multiple phone charging stations throughout the venue.
  • 📶 Wi-Fi Sponsorship - Make sure attendees stay connected while promoting your brand with a custom-branded event Wi-Fi login page.
  • 📱 Official Event App Sponsorship - Get premium ad placement and brand visibility in the expo's official app, used for schedules, exhibitor listings, and live updates.

Benefits include:

  • Your logo featured on Wi-Fi login screens, charging stations, and app pages
  • Sponsored push notifications to all attendees
  • Branded signage directing attendees to your tech stations

🎤 Educational Panel Sponsorships - Establish Thought Leadership!

Position your company as a trusted industry authority by sponsoring one of our high-impact educational panels.

  • 🎓 Cannabis Compliance & Legal Updates - Perfect for legal firms, compliance consultants, and regulatory experts.
  • 🌿 The Future of Social Equity in Cannabis - Ideal for financial institutions, social equity mentors, and advocacy groups.
  • 💼 Breaking into the Industry: Job Fair & Career Development - Ideal for staffing agencies, HR firms, and corporate sponsors looking to recruit talent.

Benefits include:

  • Brand recognition in all panel marketing
  • Speaking opportunity or brand representative on stage
  • Prominent logo placement in presentation materials

🎵 Entertainment Sponsorships - Put Your Brand in the Spotlight!

Be the exclusive sponsor of one of the expo's headline entertainment segments!

  • 🎶 Live Music Festival Sponsor - Put your brand in front of a high-energy audience by sponsoring the expo's music festival featuring local artists.
  • 🎤 DJ Stage & After-Party Sponsor - Own the vibe by branding the DJ booth & nightlife experiences throughout the event.
  • 📸 Photo & Social Media Activation Sponsorship - Feature your brand in branded photobooths and interactive social media activations.

  • Logo branding on event banners, backdrops, and concert materials
