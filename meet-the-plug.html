Bridging the Gap Between Community and Cultivators

Imagine walking into a space where cannabis culture meets commerce, where the people who grow, create, and distribute some of the best cannabis products in Massachusetts are ready to connect directly with you. Welcome to Meet the Plug, the only event designed to bridge the gap between the cannabis community and the cultivators, manufacturers, and brands shaping the industry.

For cannabis consumers, this is an exclusive opportunity to get behind the scenes, meet the minds behind their favorite products, and gain firsthand knowledge about what makes each strain, edible, or concentrate unique.

For cannabis businesses, this is the best chance to introduce your brand to the community, create direct engagement, and build customer loyalty in a way that no other event can offer.
